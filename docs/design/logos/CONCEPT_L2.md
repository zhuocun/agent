# Concept L2 — "Bracket" (dialogue / exchange)

> **Lane L2** per `DECOUPLING_ADDENDUM.md`. **Paired angular brackets** facing
> inward — call and response. No bubbles, no circles.

---

## Concept

Two bold angular chevrons / brackets `‹ ›` scaled large, nearly meeting at
center with a narrow gap — two voices in exchange. Monoline strokes with round
caps, heavy weight (≥88/512). Flat brand blue; crossing junction optional small
square (not circle) in `deep`.

---

## Geometry (512 grid)

- Left bracket: two strokes forming `‹`, stroke 88, cap round, height 360px centered
- Right bracket: mirror `›`, gap 40px at center
- Optional center square 32×32 `deep` `#14539C` at junction
- Figure ≥75% height
