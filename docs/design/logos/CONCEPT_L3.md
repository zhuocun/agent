## Concept

One vertical stem rising from bottom, forks into two angled branches at top —
a **Y-junction** or confluence glyph. Monoline stroke 88/512, round caps.
Junction marked by small **diamond** (not circle) in `deep` `#14539C`. Branches
`brand` `#2079E8` / dark `#5BA0F2`.

---

## Geometry (512 grid)

- Stem: vertical center (256, 420)→(256, 200), stroke 88
- Left branch: (256,200)→(120, 80), stroke 88
- Right branch: (256,200)→(392, 80), stroke 88
- Junction diamond: 40×40 centered (256,200), fill `deep`
- Figure ≥75% height
